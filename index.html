About

1 Legged Pigeon is the community of people working at 25 Martin Place, Sydney — home of the Australian Institute of Superannuation (AIS). Named after the resilient one-legged pigeon who frequents the building's forecourt and refuses to quit.

We are AIS members, building tenants, and friends who share a space and a city. This is our corner of the internet — minimal, intentional, and ours.
